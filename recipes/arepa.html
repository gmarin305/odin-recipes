Venezuelan Arepas

Description

Arepas are daily bread in Venezuela! This staple dish is found at almost every meal regardless if breakfast, lunch, and dinner! They can be enjoyed with warm butter, or filled with anything you'd like. Some of the most popular fillings are cheese, ham, perico (eggs with onions and tomato), avocado and chicken (reina pepiada) or black beans and white cheese(domino).
