Pan-Seared Steak

Description

This classic Steakhouse recipe is sure to satisfy your carnivorous cravings! Cook a beautiful charred and juicy steak in the pan!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • boneless ribeye steak, preferably 1 ½"" thick
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. In a medium skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil.
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. When oil is just about to smoke, add steak.
  4. Cook 7 minutes, then flip and add butter.
  5. Baste with butter and cook another 5 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 140 degrees F, for medium.
  6. Remove from pan and let rest for 5 minutes before cutting.
